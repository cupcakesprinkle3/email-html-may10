Maybe you’ve read about the latest features in ThreatMapper 1.3 in CSO Online, Dark Reading, and SC Magazine. Or maybe you’re new to ThreatMapper. Either way, we invite you to see all the new features in action.

Join us live on March 29 at 10:00 AM PT to see how to:

  • Scan for 140+ different types of sensitive secrets that may have inadvertently made their way into production
  • Generate runtime SBOMs, which may drift from your at-build-time SBOMs, so you can better secure your production environments
  • See your attack paths with direct and even indirect internet exposure, including vulnerable downstream services sitting behind multiple proxies that may be otherwise hidden
ThreatMapper helps you find all of these before an attacker does.

If you’re new to ThreatMapper, it’s the open source cloud native security observability platform that helps you identify fresh vulnerabilities and sensitive secrets in production that were not known at build or deploy time.
